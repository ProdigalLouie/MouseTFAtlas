TF co-expression modules

Co-expression blocks of TFs. Thirty seven co-expresseion blocks of TFs were identified in 24 adult organs. Cosine similarity was used to evaluate the correlations between the TFs. An angle of 66.4° (Pearson’s correlation coefficient: 0.4) was used as a cutoff, and Kendall's Tau test was applied to test for significance.

Unsupervised hierarchical clustering of the 37 co-expression modules. Six clusters were grouped according to their different tissue distribution patterns.
