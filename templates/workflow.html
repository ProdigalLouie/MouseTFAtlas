Workflow

Nuclear extract (NE) were prepared from each tissue, and then the catTFRE pull down was carried out and analyzed by LC-MS/MS to obtain the primary data. (See the Methods section for details.)

The data were analyzed with a homemade platform and stored in the mouse TF atlas database.
